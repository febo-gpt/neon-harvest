Our Story

Where the Forest Meets the Workshop

DuskWoods is a small-batch woodworking studio rooted in traditional craftsmanship. From timber-frame structures to heirloom furniture, every piece is built by hand using locally sourced hardwoods and time-honored joinery. We believe the best work happens when the light goes golden and the woods grow quiet.

Products

Built to Last Generations

Custom furniture, structures, and installations crafted from sustainably harvested wood with traditional mortise-and-tenon joinery.

Custom Cabinetry

Handcrafted cabinets in walnut, cherry, and reclaimed wood. Each piece designed for your space, built to become an heirloom.

Barn Doors & Entryways

Solid wood barn doors and custom entryways built from reclaimed heart pine and hand-forged hardware.

Timber Structures

Post-and-beam barns, sheds, and outbuildings with hand-cut joinery and traditional timber-framing techniques.

Kitchen & Built-Ins

Full kitchen cabinetry with natural wood panels, brick-compatible range hoods, and built-in storage solutions.

Artisan Lanterns

Geometric wooden lanterns hand-carved from solid blocks, designed to cast warm, intricate shadow patterns.

Commercial Installations

Custom reception desks, cubby benches, and branded signage for studios, gyms, and storefronts.

Classes & Workshops

Learn the Craft

Hands-on workshops in our woodland studio. Small groups, real tools, and projects you take home.

  • Intro to Joinery

    Learn mortise-and-tenon, dovetail, and lap joints using hand tools. Build a small keepsake box to take home.

    Beginner

  • Timber Frame Basics

    Cut, fit, and raise a small timber-frame structure. Understand layout, scribing, and traditional pegged joinery.

    Intermediate

  • Wedding Arbor Build

    Design and build a custom wedding chuppah or arbor. Perfect for couples who want a meaningful, handmade ceremony piece.

    All Levels

  • Furniture Finishing

    Oil, wax, milk paint, and hand-rubbed finishes. Learn to protect and enhance the natural beauty of wood.

    All Levels
